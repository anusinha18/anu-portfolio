Streaming Demand Signal → Revenue Forecasting (Netflix vs Disney+)

Regression Forecasting Google Trends Revenue (Quarterly)
Business Question
Can online search interest help predict future revenue growth for streaming companies?
Data & Tools
Google Trends, quarterly revenue data, Excel/Python, regression modeling, visualizations
What I Did
• Collected and cleaned revenue + search trend data
• Built a regression model to test whether interest leads revenue
• Interpreted results and translated them into a business recommendation
Key Insight
Search interest can act as an early demand signal, but strength of the relationship varies by company and time period.
Recommendation
Use search trends as a supplementary forecasting signal to inform marketing allocation and quarterly planning.
